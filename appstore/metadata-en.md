## Description
Your all-in-one guide to Oahu, Hawaii!

Discover 50+ handpicked spots & restaurants with photos. Instantly see distance and drive time from your location, then navigate with Google Maps. Select multiple spots to create the perfect route plan.

FEATURES:
- 50+ spots (beaches, nature, culture, food, cafes, shopping)
- Distance & drive time from your location
- Google Maps navigation
- Multi-stop route planner
- AI mood-based suggestions ("relaxing beach", "Instagram spots")
- Category & area filters
- Rating rankings
- Tip calculator & currency converter
- Weather-based recommendations
- Coupons & deals
- Japanese/English bilingual
- Offline support (PWA)

PERFECT FOR:
- First-time Hawaii visitors
- Families planning efficient itineraries
- Foodies looking for local gems
- Budget-conscious travelers

Make your Hawaii trip smarter and more enjoyable.

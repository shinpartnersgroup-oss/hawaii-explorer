Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Overview

Oahu Explorer ("the App") is developed by Shin Partners Inc. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

Location Data: The App uses your device's location to calculate distances to spots. Location data is processed on your device only and is never sent to our servers.

Usage Data: The App stores browsing history, search history, and preferences locally on your device using localStorage. This data is never transmitted externally.

No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Services

The App contains links to third-party booking services (Viator, Booking.com, Klook, RentalCars.com). When you click these links, you leave our App and their privacy policies apply.

Images are loaded from Unsplash and Wikimedia Commons. These services may log image requests per their own policies.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. You can clear all data by clearing your browser cache or uninstalling the App.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page.
